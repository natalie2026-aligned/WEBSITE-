Nerve health support
Built for your situation
These are adapted to sciatica and lower limb nerve pain. Do what you can, skip what aggravates. Report anything new to Dr. Chen.
🦶
Ankle alphabet
3 min daily · Seated
  • Sit with leg extended, foot off the floor
  • Trace each letter A-Z with your big toe
  • Go slowly -- this is nerve activation, not a workout
  • Switch feet. Note any difference between sides.
🧘
Sciatic nerve floss
5 min daily · Seated or lying
  • Sit tall, extend one leg straight
  • Flex foot (toes toward you) and hold 3 seconds
  • Point foot away and hold 3 seconds
  • Repeat 10 times each side -- stop if sharp pain
✋
Hand dexterity
2 min daily · Any time
  • Tap each finger to thumb, one at a time
  • Repeat in reverse order
  • Try with eyes closed to increase challenge
  • Both hands -- note any asymmetry
💨
Vagus nerve breathing
5 min · Any time · Especially useful on hard days
  • Inhale for 4 counts through the nose
  • Hold for 2 counts
  • Exhale slowly for 8 counts through the mouth
  • The long exhale activates the vagus nerve directly
