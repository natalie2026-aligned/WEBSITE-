Clinical references worth reading
Mersfelder & Nichols 2016 — Gabapentin: Abuse, Dependence, and Withdrawal
The most-cited peer-reviewed paper on gabapentin withdrawal risk. Established the over-1,800mg daily threshold. Worth reading before any conversation with your doctor.
PubMed
2025 JAGS Case Study — Patient-Directed Gabapentin Taper
Recent case study documenting a successful supervised taper with patient-directed pacing. Useful evidence to bring to a doctor who is uncertain about slow reduction.
JAGS 2025
Deprescribing.org
Evidence-based deprescribing guidelines and tools for patients and clinicians. Good background reading and helpful for practitioners who haven't encountered formal deprescribing protocols.
Clinical
Worth reading
Gabapentin Withdrawal: One Year Later — Mad In America
A first-person account of completing a long gabapentin taper. One of the most honest, detailed accounts of the full experience online. The community comments are worth reading too.

Mayo Clinic Connect — Tapering Off Gabapentin
Forum thread with real patient experiences tapering gabapentin. More moderated and clinically grounded than most social media groups. Good signal-to-noise ratio.

Why Most Gabapentin Tapers Fail — GabaDown Blog
The patterns that derail most tapers — and what the ones that succeed have in common. The most useful thing I've written about this process.
