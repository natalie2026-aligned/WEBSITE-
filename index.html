The supplement protocol

Natural support — evidence-informed, specifically timed

Every supplement in the GabaDown protocol has a specific reason for being there. Here's what we use and why.

Lion's Mane Mushroom
Stimulates nerve growth factor (NGF) — the protein that repairs and regrows nerve fibres. Addresses actual nerve damage rather than masking pain.
Morning
Alpha-Lipoic Acid (ALA)
Strongest evidence-backed supplement for neuropathy. A universal antioxidant protecting nerve cells in both fat and water-soluble tissue. Reduces burning and numbness.
Morning with food
B12 Methylcobalamin
B12 deficiency directly worsens nerve pain. The methylcobalamin form is what the nervous system actually uses. Supports myelin sheath repair — the insulation around nerve fibres.
Morning
Turmeric / Curcumin
Potent anti-inflammatory that calms the nerve tissue irritation underlying chronic pain. Take with black pepper extract — increases absorption by up to 2000%.
Morning with food
Ashwagandha (KSM-66)
A GABA-A receptor activator — directly overlaps with what gabapentin does, helping the nervous system stay calm as doses drop. Lowers cortisol that spikes during withdrawal.
Midday
Omega-3 Fish Oil
Reduces neuroinflammation and supports nerve cell membrane integrity. EPA has strong evidence for reducing depression and anxiety that accompanies dose reductions.
Midday with food
Reishi Mushroom
Triterpenes interact with the GABA pathway — promoting calm and sleep through the same system gabapentin affects. A natural nervous system regulator. Takes 2–4 weeks to build.
Evening
Magnesium Glycinate
Supports GABA receptors, eases muscle tension, reduces nerve hypersensitivity, and dramatically improves sleep. Space 2–3 hours away from all gabapentin doses.
Evening — away from gaba
L-Theanine
Promotes calm focused alpha brain waves without sedation. Fast-acting. Reduces anxiety and racing thoughts that spike in the first days after each dose reduction.
Evening
CBD Oil (Full Spectrum)
Bridges the pain gap as gabapentin doses come down. Sublingual absorption. Full-spectrum outperforms isolate for nerve pain. Also supports sleep and reduces withdrawal anxiety.
Morning and evening
Valerian Root
Increases GABA by inhibiting the enzymes that break it down. Use as needed during the rough patch after each dose drop — especially nights 2–5 at a new lower dose.
Bedtime as needed
